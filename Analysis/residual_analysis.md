# Residual Analysis

Residual = Actual Sales − Predicted Sales

Positive residuals indicate stores where actual sales were higher than predicted.

Negative residuals indicate stores where actual sales were lower than predicted.

The largest positive residuals may represent stores that benefited from factors not included in the regression model.

The largest negative residuals may indicate operational issues, local competition, seasonal effects, or other business factors not captured in the model.

Overall, the residuals suggest that while regression explains a portion of sales variation, additional business variables could improve prediction accuracy.
