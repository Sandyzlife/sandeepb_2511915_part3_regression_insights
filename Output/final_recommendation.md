# Final Recommendation

## Executive Summary
Regression analysis was performed to identify the factors associated with monthly sales.

## Key Findings
- Footfall showed the strongest relationship with monthly sales.
- Marketing spend also had a statistically significant positive relationship.
- The multiple regression model was statistically significant but explained less variation than the strongest simple model.

## Business Recommendation
Leadership should prioritize initiatives that increase store footfall while continuing targeted marketing investments.

Marketing campaigns should be evaluated together with operational improvements rather than in isolation.

Regression identifies statistical associations but does not prove that one variable directly causes changes in monthly sales.

## Limitations
- Other external factors were not included.
- Store-specific and seasonal influences may affect results.
- Additional predictors could improve future models.
