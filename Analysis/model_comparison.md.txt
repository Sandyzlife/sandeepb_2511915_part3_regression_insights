# Model Comparison

## Model 1 - Marketing Spend
- Dependent Variable: Monthly Sales
- Independent Variable: Marketing Spend
- R² = 0.1672
- P-value = 2.48E-14
- Marketing spend has a statistically significant positive relationship with monthly sales.

## Model 2 - Footfall
- Dependent Variable: Monthly Sales
- Independent Variable: Footfall
- R² = 0.7363
- P-value = 4.75E-94
- This model explains the largest proportion of variation in monthly sales and is the strongest simple regression model.

## Multiple Regression
- Dependent Variable: Monthly Sales
- Multiple numerical predictors were included.
- R² = 0.0381
- Overall model is statistically significant (p = 0.00044).
- The model indicates that multiple business factors jointly influence sales, although explanatory power is lower than the strongest simple model.

## Final Model Selected
The Footfall model provides the highest explanatory power and is the most useful for understanding monthly sales in this analysis.
